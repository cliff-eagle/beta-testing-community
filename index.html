A community of beta testers exploring the **Agent-Aided Pedagogical Model (AAPM)** within immersive life-simulation environments.

Community Purpose

This community connects researchers and students to test dialogic agents that foster critical thinking. Your feedback directly impacts the evolution of the AAPM framework.

The AAPM Framework

Our model leverages dialogic AI agents to guide learners through complex social simulations. Testers explore how these agents facilitate *scaffolded inquiry* in a life-sim environment.
